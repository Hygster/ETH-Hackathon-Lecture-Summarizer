About TLDR; Lectures

TLDR; Lectures is a web app that provides a text summary of a lecture in customizable granularity. It was developed in October, 2023, in Viscon Hackathon by five ETHZ students - Kristijonas Bučiūnas, Fabius Grünhagen, Jonathan Manz, August Rønberg and Lorin Urbantat.
